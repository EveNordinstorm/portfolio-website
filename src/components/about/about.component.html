About Me

Experience

Starting my career in Graphic Design, I quickly branched out into additional skills to complement my position. In more than five years of experience, I have been using Adobe Creative Cloud daily, marketing through social media, SEO and analytics, as well as IT support for internal and external users and maintaining enrolment and website code.

Development Process

Building upon this, my process for developing projects starts with a strong sense of design and user experience. The planning stage always starts with visual design, bringing together branding, layout and functionality of components. Once I've wireframed my website layout, I draw from my tech stack and research new programming tools to construct a robust application to suit my design and functionality aims. As much as the UI is vitally important, something I'm passionate about is constructing my code in an organised and effective way to create code that is maintainable, runs efficiently and is easy to read.
