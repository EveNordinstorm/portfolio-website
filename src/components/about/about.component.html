My Story

My career began with a passion for visual design, and I have consistently sought out new technologies to enhance my skills. With proven experience in creating high-quality content using a range of digital tools, I’ve grown confident by challenging myself to manage projects, liaise with clients, and provide technical support. Now, I’m transitioning into web development, combining my expertise in design with both frontend and backend development, equipping me with the skills to excel as a well-rounded programmer.

Development Process

My development process begins with a focus on user experience and the integration of branding into my design. I start by wireframing the website's structure and components, ensuring that usability and project goals are well defined before diving into the technical aspects.

  • Artistry Net Wireframe
  • Equipped After Dark Wireframe
  • Jet Set Wireframe

With the wireframe as a guide, I apply my technical knowledge, as well as researching new tools and technologies, to construct robust applications that meet both design and functionality objectives. While the frontend design is essential, I place equal importance on writing clean, organised and maintainable code, ensuring that the backend is just as efficient and scalable. This approach allows me to create projects that are not only visually elegant but functionally effective.
